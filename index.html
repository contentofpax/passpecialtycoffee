Our Story

A small bar built
around one perfect pour.

Born in 2026, PAS Specialty Coffee is a purely vegetarian café nestled on Agonda Beach Road, one of Goa's most serene and unspoilt stretches of coast.

We built this place around a single obsession: authentic specialty coffee, done right, every single time. From ceremonial-grade matcha to signature cold brews, every cup is made with purpose, paired with a short, considered all-day kitchen.

Known for brilliant coffee, genuinely warm hospitality, and a space that makes you want to stay a little longer.

The Bar

Precision tools.

Every detail at PAS is intentional: from the espresso machine to the handpicked ceramics. We serve specialty-grade arabica, ceremonial matcha, and cold brews crafted over time.

Our baristas treat every cup as a small piece of craft. No shortcuts. No noise. Just coffee the way it should be.
